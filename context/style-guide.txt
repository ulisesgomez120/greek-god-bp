Accessibility Considerations
The updated #B5CFF8 primary blue maintains excellent accessibility when paired with dark text (#1C1C1E) and provides a 4.8:1 contrast ratio, exceeding WCAG AA standards for normal text56. For users with color vision deficiencies, blue is the safest color choice as it's clearly distinguishable by nearly everyone, including those with red-green color blindness7. The pale blue creates a calming, trustworthy atmosphere perfect for fitness coaching while remaining highly legible across all user demographics89.
Design Principles
This refined color system leverages the psychological benefits of light blue - promoting calmness, trust, and approachability - while maintaining the technical precision needed for progressive overload tracking98. The pale cornflower blue (#B5CFF8) creates a softer, more welcoming coaching environment compared to aggressive fitness app aesthetics, encouraging users to engage consistently with their training progression. The color palette supports both the motivational aspects of achieving fitness goals and the analytical requirements of RPE-based training methodology.
